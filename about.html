About me

Oyewale Tayo is a front-end developer and IT specialist who's keen on changing the world through innovation and technology. I've always been an avid lover of everything technology from mobile technology to cloud computing. After four years of studying Computer Science at the Tai Solarin University of Education, and getting my Bachelor's degree, I worked in the manufacturing and banking industry but none was satisfactory enough; I needed to be behind a computer, creating innovative solutions, beautifying the web, and contributing to the safety of the infrastructures that make up the internet. So, I hopped on Udemy, got some web development courses, and started coding my nights away. And that's how my journey as a front-end developer and an IT specialist started. I'm now a Google-certified IT specialist with a solid knowledge of how to create high-end front-end solutions for a wide range of industries. My proficiency lies in developing and testing web-based applications using different technologies, including html , CSS , JavaScript, React, Node Js and PostgreSQL. I plan to combine my technical skills with my broad background to create impactful solutions that'll change the world.
